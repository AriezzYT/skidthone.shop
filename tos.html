Terms of Service

Software guidelines

- Only debug the software with http debugger/fiddler. Theres no debugging protection for that, you are free to track requests Ethone performs. Everything else if not permitted and will face consequences.
- License sharing is strongly not advised and will result with instant consequences. Only your main and alt accounts are allowed to use Ethone. (Dont open 2 instances of Ethone at the same time with different tokens)
- Dont perform heavy actions in a short period of time. Although Ethone is very safe to use, nuking 20 servers in half an hour surely wont help your ratelimit. Use responsibly.
- Selfbots are against discords tos. Dont get caught sending embeds in a public server. You will get reported.
- Dont share the software or a discord invite. Non-compliance = consequences.
- If you notice a strong ratelimit when sending messages, close Ethone and dont interact with discord for a few minutes. Then you are free to continue.
- If you arent a reseller of Ethone, selling/trading it will result in you facing consequences and the keys you are selling being deleted.

Community rules

- Dont self-advertise/advertise
- You dont have to be friendly, just dont be annoying and toxic.
- Dont spam/raid/annoy
- Dont expose other members for using a selfbot.
- No doxxing.

Logging

When talking about logs theres 2 different types, storing them and temp receiving them.
- Your Ethone account: Username, email, license key, date registered, last id and uuid. Password is hashed. (stored)
- Authenticating: Auth credentials, time, IP address. (not stored)
- Purchasing through sellix: Invoice ID, gateway, email, IP address (stored on sellix.io)

Information

- Ethone has only been tested to fully work on windows 10. Some people also said it works with Linux wine.
- Staff is able to break these tos themselves and take actions against people who break them.
